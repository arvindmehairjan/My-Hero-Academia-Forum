About

MHA Ground is a forum where fictional characters of the anime My Hero Academia can create post, update and delete them. They can create their own account, login and logout. The purpose of this app is to share content with other people.

This is a fictional and non-commercial application. This app is as a fan & hobby project and is not monetized is any way.
